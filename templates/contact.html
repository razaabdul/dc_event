Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our wedding planning services

How far in advance should I book your services?

We recommend booking our services at least 6-12 months in advance, especially for peak wedding seasons. However, we can also accommodate shorter timelines based on availability.

Do you provide services outside Mumbai?

Yes, we provide destination wedding services across India and internationally. Additional travel and accommodation costs may apply for outstation events.

Can I customize the wedding packages?

All our packages are customizable. We work closely with you to create a personalized package that fits your vision and budget.

What is included in the photography package?

Our photography packages include pre-wedding shoots, wedding day coverage, edited high-resolution photos, online gallery, and physical prints. Videography can be added as an additional service.

Do you handle vendor coordination?

Yes, we handle all vendor coordination including florists, musicians, transportation, and other service providers. We have a network of trusted vendors we work with regularly.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and digital payment methods. Payment is typically structured in installments with an advance booking amount.
